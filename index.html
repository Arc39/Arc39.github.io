A Social Music Experience


DISCOTEK

Discotek is a social music platform that allows users connect over their love of music. Users have creative control to create listening floors which stream music tailored to their preferences.

Features

With Discotek, your floor is your dance floor. Upon entering the floor choose to join an existing floor or create your own. With floor creation, choose from a variety of genres to suit your musical tastes. Inside your chosen floor listen to music with friends simultaneously, chat a little, and check out upcoming songs.

Tools

A variety of tools were used in the creation of Discotek.

Future plans

Upon opening the app you may choose to login with Facebook or google. After logging in you will be redirected to our floor page.
