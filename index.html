Discotek

A Social Music Experience


DISCOTEK


Discotek is a social music platform that allows users connect over their love of music. Users have creative control to create listening floors which stream music tailored to their preferences.

Features


With Discotek, your floor is your dance floor. Upon entering the floor choose to join an existing floor or create your own. With floor creation, choose from a variety of genres to suit your musical tastes. Inside your chosen floor listen to music with friends simultaneously, chat a little, and check out upcoming songs.

Creating Discotek


Discotek is a platform that consists of three levels. The three levels include a backend server that holds all data needed for the application, a middle-ware android service that handles data exchanges between the server and client, and the android application that users interact with directly.

Why Discotek?


This application came into creation as a solution to a problem. It came to be out of an initial idea for a silent disco application. The idea was to allow users control over genre-themed floors in order to allow others to connect to the the floor and join in on the party. Music is synced to up to a second difference to allow users to enjoy music while in tune with others. The application also allows for users to communicate with each other without the need to remove their headphones.
